Health

Health & Wellness Technology

Building consumer and clinical health applications that empower individuals to take charge of their nutrition, holistic wellness, and chronic care — putting evidence-based health tools directly in the hands of users.

01 FODO Nutrition App

FODO is your personal nutrition companion. It helps users track meals, understand macros, set dietary goals, and build sustainable eating habits — all through an intuitive mobile experience. Whether you're managing weight, optimizing athletic performance, or addressing specific dietary needs, FODO puts evidence-based nutrition guidance in your pocket.

Meal Tracking
Log every meal with a comprehensive food database. Scan barcodes or search thousands of foods for fast, accurate logging.
Macro & Calorie Analysis
Real-time breakdown of calories, carbohydrates, protein, and fats. Know exactly what you're consuming and how it maps to your goals.
Dietary Goal Setting
Set personalized targets based on your health objectives — weight loss, muscle gain, maintenance, or condition-specific needs.
Progress Insights
Visual trends, weekly summaries, and actionable recommendations that keep you informed and motivated on your nutrition journey.

Key Features

Meal Logging Barcode Scanner Macro Tracking Calorie Goals Food Database Progress Charts Daily Insights
02 LAFIAKALO Holistic Wellness App

LAFIAKALO takes a 360° view of wellbeing — integrating mind, body, sleep, stress, and spiritual health into a unified wellness experience. Rooted in the philosophy that true health goes beyond the physical, LAFIAKALO guides users toward balanced living through personalized insights, daily practices, and whole-person care.

Holistic Wellness Score
A single, unified score that reflects the full picture of your wellbeing — mental, physical, spiritual, and restorative health combined.
Mental Health Tracking
Monitor mood, stress levels, and emotional wellbeing over time. Build awareness and healthy mental habits through guided check-ins.
Sleep Analytics
Track sleep quality, duration, and patterns. Receive personalized recommendations to improve rest and recovery.
Daily Wellness Practices
Guided meditations, breathwork, movement prompts, and mindfulness exercises tailored to your current wellness state.

Key Features

Wellness Score Mood Tracking Sleep Monitor Stress Management Mindfulness Body Tracking Spiritual Health
03 Dialysis Flow Dialysis Session App

Dialysis Flow is a dedicated mobile companion for dialysis patients. It helps patients log and track individual dialysis sessions, monitor vitals, record symptoms, manage fluid intake between sessions, and share session reports with their care team — putting patients in control of their treatment journey and improving clinical communication.

Session Logging
Record every dialysis session with detailed notes on duration, machine settings, and how you felt before and after treatment.
Vitals Tracking
Log blood pressure, heart rate, weight, and temperature pre- and post-session to build a comprehensive clinical picture over time.
Fluid Intake Monitor
Track daily fluid consumption against your prescribed limit. Get gentle reminders to stay within safe intake targets between sessions.
Care Team Reports
Generate and share structured session summaries directly with your nephrologist or dialysis care team for better-informed clinical decisions.

Key Features

Session Logging Vitals Tracking Fluid Intake Monitor Symptom Journal Care Team Reports Trend Charts
