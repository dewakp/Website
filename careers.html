That means we need extraordinary people across every function: the engineers who architect the future, the marketers who make it land, the lawyers who protect it, and the finance minds who scale it.

Engineering
Creative Engineers Software · Mobile · Platform · AI

We need engineers who don't just write code — they architect solutions, question assumptions, and build products that are genuinely elegant. You will work across our health apps (FODO, LAFIAKALO, Dialysis Flow), our cyber risk platforms (CyberRisk Architecture OS, CRAM Terminals), and whatever we dream up next.

You might be our person if you…
Get excited by hard, undefined problems — not just well-scoped tickets
Have built something from scratch and shipped it to real users
Think about UX, architecture, and business impact simultaneously
Can work across mobile, web, or backend — and learn what you don't know
Bring creativity to technical design, not just technical correctness

Sales & Marketing

We are building genuinely new categories — a credit score for cyber risk, a Bloomberg terminal for security professionals, holistic wellness apps. Legal
Legal Counsel Corporate · Regulatory · IP · Compliance

Across healthcare apps, a cybersecurity platform, a financial-data-style terminal, and a consulting practice, we navigate a complex web of regulation, intellectual property, and commercial agreements. We need legal counsel who is sharp, practical, and genuinely embedded in the business — not a distant risk-averse gatekeeper, but a strategic partner.

You might be our person if you…
Have experience with health tech (HIPAA), cybersecurity regulation, or SaaS contracting
Know how to protect IP while moving fast — not slow everything down
Can draft, negotiate, and advise across corporate, commercial, and regulatory domains
Speak business, not just legalese — and earn trust across the leadership team
Are energized by novel legal questions, not just template application

Finance
Finance Guru Strategy · FP&A · Fundraising · Operations

We are scaling multiple companies under one group structure — each with its own economics, revenue model, and growth trajectory. We need a finance leader who can hold the full picture: building the models, controlling the cash, telling the story to investors, and making sure every dollar is working as hard as we are.

You might be our person if you…
Have built financial models for SaaS, health tech, or multi-entity companies
Have experience with startup fundraising — decks, diligence, term sheets
Can do the analysis and explain it clearly to a non-finance executive team
Bring structure and discipline without bureaucracy or friction
Are excited by the challenge of scaling multiple P&Ls simultaneously
