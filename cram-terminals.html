Cyber Risk Intelligence

Bloomberg Terminal meets Reddit — purpose-built for cyber risk professionals. Real-time risk data, live intelligence feeds, EISSM scoring, and a credentialed professional community, all in one platform.

The Concept

Two Great
Platforms.
One Purpose.

The financial world has Bloomberg — a professional terminal that gives analysts, traders, and executives real-time data, analytics, and news in one place. The internet has Reddit — a community platform where professionals share knowledge, debate ideas, and build collective intelligence. CRAM Terminals brings both of these concepts together, exclusively for the cyber risk industry.

Bloomberg The Terminal Side

Real-time EISSM scores, sector risk indices, threat data feeds, regulatory alerts, and enterprise benchmarking — all in a high-density professional dashboard built for serious cyber risk work.

Reddit The Community Side

A credentialed professional network where Analysts, CISOs, Auditors, and Regulators discuss threat intelligence, share methodologies, debate risk postures, and build the industry's collective knowledge.
